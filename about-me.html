About Me!

Mahjoobah Faizi, a native of Herat, Afghanistan, nurtured a passion for English language and literature, pursuing her studies at Herat University until the Taliban’s ban on women’s education interrupted her senior year. Undeterred, she continues to cultivate her love of writing, crafting short stories published on an international platform. An avid reader, particularly of novels, she counts Elif Shafak among her favorite authors. In her free time, she explores the worlds of design and coding, further expanding her creative skillset.

Driven by a desire to refine her English fluency and achieve native-level proficiency, Mahjoobah envisions a future as an accomplished author. Her aspirations also include mastering coding to become a skilled web developer and designer, ultimately securing a stable and fulfilling career. Despite the obstacles she has faced, Mahjoobah remains dedicated to her passions, demonstrating resilience and a commitment to her personal and professional growth.
